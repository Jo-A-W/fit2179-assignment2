🌾 Australia's Food Trade Story

How Australia feeds the world — and what the world sends back.

Overview

The Big Picture

Australia is one of the world's largest food exporters, sending over $66 billion worth of food products overseas in 2025. This section shows how the total value of food exports has grown over two decades.

Exports

What Australia Exports

From beef to wheat, Australia exports a wide variety of food products to markets across Asia, the Middle East, and beyond.

Regional Breakdown

State by State

Different states specialise in different food products. Western Australia leads in wheat, Queensland in beef, and Tasmania in seafood.

Trends

How Trade Has Shifted Over Time

Australia's trading relationships have shifted significantly over the past two decades. China has grown to become Australia's largest food export market.
